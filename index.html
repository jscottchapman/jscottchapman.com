Work That Worked

TechFix SMS: Doubling Engagement With Personalized Texts

2025

A client came to me and said, roughly: "What if we texted students reminders to study?" That was the entire brief.

I dug in and discovered the real problem. Students had knowledge gaps nobody was tracking. Outreach was generic and sporadic. Nothing scaled. So I built an automated platform that categorized students by their weakest subject, sent personalized daily training links at 7 AM, tracked delivery across dual SMS providers, and gave admins a dashboard to see everything in real time.

2x student engagement
98%+ SMS delivery rate
10 hrs/wk admin time saved
"Scott has a rare ability to bridge the gap between an idea and the final product. His contributions were instrumental to the success of our program." Lowell Greene, TechFix / ATG

Morphose: 3D Physical Therapy, HIPAA and All

2019 – 2022

Physical therapists were handing patients printed sheets with stick-figure exercises and hoping for the best. I built Morphose: a HIPAA-compliant platform with a library of 850+ interactive 3D exercise animations, real-time progress tracking, secure messaging, and therapist dashboards for prescription management. The whole thing replaced a paper handout with a system that actually shows you how to move your body correctly.

850+ 3D exercise animations
HIPAA fully compliant
Sole developer, full stack

Continuous Glucose Monitor: Device to Dashboard

2019

At Estenda Solutions, I built the visualization layer for a proprietary continuous glucose monitor. The device talked BLE to a phone, the phone pushed data to the cloud, and I built what the doctor saw on the other end: longitudinal glucose trends, time-series readings, and the clinical context to make treatment decisions. The full pipeline from a sensor on your arm to a chart on your physician's screen.

BLE device-to-cloud pipeline
Real-time glucose visualization
